    Jonas Dawn¶

    Healer of Timberfall — Church of the Dawn

    Overview¶

    The closest thing Timberfall has to a doctor, and by his own cheerful admission not a brave man. Jonas comes from a large family that has been in town a long time and runs the meager little temple here. He spends his days patching up loggers who cut their own thumbs off doing basic work at the treeline, and he is very clear that he considers most of them idiots. He is also, right now, falling apart.

    Appearance¶

    White robes — the vestments of his church — badly stained with alcohol and worn far too long without washing. Around his neck a pendant: a white field bearing a half-risen sun with small rays coming off it. Dark-skinned. When the party found him at the Fat Goat he had an ale at his side and one leg shaking so hard under the table that a drow across the room could hear it.

    Personality¶

    Nervous, profane, self-aware, and genuinely kind underneath it. He describes himself, without much shame, as "too much of a pansy" to go into the Graylight after his own sister. He calls himself a do-gooder and then immediately clarifies that unlike Miriam he is not an idiot about it. His grief comes out sideways, as complaining.

    He does not have much, and he gave away what he had anyway.

    Goals¶

    Get Miriam back. Everything else is noise.

    Relationships¶

    • Miriam Dawn — his sister. She went into the Graylight about a week ago with three others to rescue the missing loggers. He begged her not to go. She went. He is coming apart over it.
    • Icarus — an old friend, or was. Greeted him warmly by name across the tavern. Within a few minutes Icarus had him by the shirt, promising to kill him if he didn't talk. Jonas's parting shot — "the dawn gives me power" — was not entirely a joke, and he made a point of handing the party's healing potions to other people, with instructions not to waste one on the human.
    • Old Marnie — supplies him with potions and helps him brew them. Jonas sent the party on to Marnie for real information about the forest.
    • Wilonet — bought his goodwill back after Icarus burned it, and promised to try to bring his sister home.

    Session History¶

    Twelve Missing — Found shaking alone at the Fat Goat. Told the party about the first rescue party and his sister's part in it, and that Miriam had negotiated thirty-five gold a survivor where the party had settled for twenty. Knew nothing useful about the forest itself and said so — "they said that place is scary, so I just never fucking went in there." Was assaulted by Icarus, defended by the other two, and gave up healing potions from the church regardless. Pointed the party at Old Marnie. Sent Icarus off with "may the light find you," which Icarus did not care for at all — and which something inside Icarus liked even less.

    Open Questions¶

    • Is he holding something back? Two separate insight reads came up the same: genuinely terrified, genuinely worried about his sister. Whether that's the whole truth is another matter.
    • He pocketed a second potion rather than hand it over. He is not quite as empty-handed as he claims.
