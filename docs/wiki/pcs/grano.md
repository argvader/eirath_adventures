# Grano

### **Dwarf Cleric — Last of his Monastery**

## Overview

Ninety-five years old, gray-haired, and very quiet. Chainmail, a mace, and a shield, carried with what can only be described as a soft-spoken elegance — he is not a loud man and does not become one under pressure. When the party first gathered at the job board it was Grano who actually asked the useful question: *how long have these twelve been missing?*

He mostly grumbles. The party has already worked out that when he does say something, it is worth hearing.

## What He Carries

Grano spent the last seven years alone. He is the last survivor of a monastery somewhere up in the **Cinderspire Peaks**, where everyone else was killed. He mentioned this once, flatly, to a stranger who had irritated him, and then said nothing further about it.

He has not explained what happened there. Nobody has asked.

## Faith

His holy magic is **bright, vibrant, cleansing, and plain** — his own words. His **sacred flame** falls on a thing and leaves it charred. In a forest full of gray fog and lying half-light, he is the only light in the party that is actually light.

He knew the [Church of the Dawn](../factions/church-of-the-dawn.md) on sight, and recognized its sigil and its theology of redemption and rebirth immediately — a very good roll on a subject he clearly already knew well. Which god he himself serves has not yet been said aloud.

## Temperament

Disinclined to suffer fools, and unbothered about hiding it. Told the [Silverbrier head of security](../npcs/silverbrier-head-of-security.md), to his face and without heat, *"I don't like you at all."* Thinks the whole logging enterprise is greed dressed up as commerce — steel is expensive, the war was foolishness, and these people rush off to danger without a thought for anyone involved.

## Relationships

- **Wilonet** — mutual respect, arrived at almost instantly. They conferred in Dwarvish about whether the job was worth taking before either of them had said a friendly word to the human.
- **Icarus** — has said essentially nothing to him. When [Jonas Dawn](../npcs/jonas-dawn.md) asked Grano to vouch for Icarus, Grano answered honestly: *"I don't know him that well."*
- **[Jonas Dawn](../npcs/jonas-dawn.md)** — introduced himself politely, asked the real question about why Miriam had gone, and was the one Jonas trusted with a healing potion — along with the instruction not to waste it on Icarus.

## Session History

**[Twelve Missing](../../sessions/2026-07-05.md)** — Asked the questions that mattered and let others do the shouting. Identified the Church of the Dawn from its potions and pendant. Scouted the party's route into the [Graylight](../locations/graylight-forest.md), spotting the cart tracks that marked the loggers' path. Took a wolf's jaws on his armor without much complaint, burned it with sacred flame, and cracked it with his mace.
