# Icarus

**Player:** Joe · **Ancestry:** Human · **Class:** Warlock

*Mercenary*

## Overview

A soldier-for-hire in his mid-thirties who has plainly had a hard, ugly life and wants you to know it. Broad-shouldered and stout, with a heavy scar carved across his face and one eye left solid white where it passed. Stained black leather, a long black cloak worn thrown back — deliberately, so the scar shows. He does not like people. He says so, repeatedly, without prompting.

He has worked for the [Silverbrier family](../factions/silverbrier-family.md) before and has no illusions about them: they hand out the dangerous jobs, they never pay properly, and they never train anyone. He was supposed to have been the security escort for the twelve loggers now missing in the [Graylight Forest](../locations/graylight-forest.md). He turned up two weeks late. They went in without him.

He has not stopped insisting that this is *their* fault.

## The Voice

Icarus's patron has not been named. But when [Jonas Dawn](../npcs/jonas-dawn.md) offered him the blessing of the [Church of the Dawn](../factions/church-of-the-dawn.md) — *"may the light find you"* — and Icarus answered that he didn't care about the light, something in his head answered too. A thought that arrived on its own, in a voice that was not his:

> *Good. We don't want the light.*

He has told no one. See **[The Voice](../npcs/the-voice.md)** for everything it has said since.

## What He Did on the Road

The [Graylight Veil](../locations/graylight-forest.md) pulled it out of him on the fourth day
in, unasked: the days before he ever reached [Timberfall](../locations/timberfall.md), sleeping
rough, with a voice in his skull repeating that it needed **sustenance** and that they were
bound together. And then the farmhouse, and the kindly old man who fed him, refused his gold,
and said it had been nice to have someone to talk to again.

The voice woke him at two in the morning and sent him down to the cellar, where
[the farmer](../npcs/the-old-farmer.md) was chopping up what looked like a human body with arms
that were suddenly far too strong for him. *Kill him. Slice his throat and sustain me.* Icarus
kicked him down the stairs, drew his sword — and his other hand came up on its own and his
magic went off for **the first time in his life** and took the old man's face off.

Then the blood ran up the blade and vanished into it, and the arms on the corpse were an old
man's arms again, and the body on the block was a **deer**. And the voice told him they would
be doing this again, and that if they didn't, **his mother's life is forfeit.**

He came out of the flashback shaking in front of the other two. Asked if he was all right, he
said he was fine and that he was just thinking of the past. That is still all he has said:

> *I saw my past. The moment I don't speak of. Never.*

## In Combat

His magic comes out the color of the forest he is standing in — his **eldritch blast** looks like he has reached down, scooped a handful of the Graylight's fog off the ground, and flung it. Slight gray. It barely shows against the mist.

He carries a bow he keeps reaching for out of habit, over spells that would serve him better. He is fragile — eleven hit points, an armor class of eleven, and a wolf took seven of those hit points out of him in a single bite, on the arm he casts from. He spent the back half of the fight behind a tree.

He learned from it. Against the Nightroot Asp his **first action of the fight** was
**armor of Agathys**, cast before he even knew what he was fighting — *"since I'm smarter this
fight."* He described it as frost: a layer over him like breath fogging a window, seen through
but not clearly. He also learned the hard way what an attack of opportunity is, by trying to
back away from a snake mid-melee.

His **witch bolt** is red — deep red, with little sparks of yellow light trying to push
through the color from underneath. It is the first magic of his that looks like anything at
all, and it is the thing that killed the asp.

**Known spells:** eldritch blast, armor of Agathys, green-flame blade, witch bolt.

## Relationships

- **Wilonet** — mutual, enthusiastic loathing, wearing thin in places. She threw a rock at a snake, missed, and hit him in the eye with it; he told her she was an idiot and she announced she would stand back and let them handle it. Then he made a joke about the dwarf who had just bled out on the ground and *she* was the one who let it go — *"I'm gonna let that slide."* When she apologized for missing her shots, it was to Grano, not to him.
- **Grano** — barely spoken, and now considerably more owed. The dwarf opened the session healing Icarus's mauled arm, and Icarus's entire response was *"Grano. Thank you, Grano."* When the asp put Grano on the ground with his throat open, Icarus killed it. The two of them still do not talk.
- **[Jonas Dawn](../npcs/jonas-dawn.md)** — an old friend, greeted warmly by name. Icarus grabbed him by the shirt and threatened to kill him for information within minutes of sitting down.
- **The [Silverbrier head of security](../npcs/silverbrier-head-of-security.md)** — a running, mutual contempt. Icarus cannot negotiate with him, because every conversation ends with *"you were two weeks late."*
