# Icarus

**Player:** Joe · **Ancestry:** Human · **Class:** Warlock

*Mercenary*

## Overview

A soldier-for-hire in his mid-thirties who has plainly had a hard, ugly life and wants you to know it. Broad-shouldered and stout, with a heavy scar carved across his face and one eye left solid white where it passed. Stained black leather, a long black cloak worn thrown back — deliberately, so the scar shows. He does not like people. He says so, repeatedly, without prompting.

He has worked for the [Silverbrier family](../factions/silverbrier-family.md) before and has no illusions about them: they hand out the dangerous jobs, they never pay properly, and they never train anyone. He was supposed to have been the security escort for the twelve loggers now missing in the [Graylight Forest](../locations/graylight-forest.md). He turned up two weeks late. They went in without him.

He has not stopped insisting that this is *their* fault.

## The Voice

Icarus's patron has not been named. But when [Jonas Dawn](../npcs/jonas-dawn.md) offered him the blessing of the [Church of the Dawn](../factions/church-of-the-dawn.md) — *"may the light find you"* — and Icarus answered that he didn't care about the light, something in his head answered too. A thought that arrived on its own, in a voice that was not his:

> *Good. We don't want the light.*

He has told no one.

## In Combat

His magic comes out the color of the forest he is standing in — his **eldritch blast** looks like he has reached down, scooped a handful of the Graylight's fog off the ground, and flung it. Slight gray. It barely shows against the mist.

He carries a bow he keeps reaching for out of habit, over spells that would serve him better. He is fragile — eleven hit points, an armor class of eleven, and a wolf took seven of those hit points out of him in a single bite, on the arm he casts from. He spent the back half of the fight behind a tree.

**Known spells:** eldritch blast, armor of Agathys, green-flame blade, witch bolt.

## Relationships

- **Wilonet** — mutual, enthusiastic loathing. She saved him from walking into one of her own hunter's traps, then triggered it with a rock to show him what he'd nearly stepped in. He thanked her. She respected him fractionally more for it. He still hates her exactly as much.
- **Grano** — barely spoken. Icarus has decided, on no evidence, that he likes the dwarf so far. The two of them have said almost nothing to each other and both seem content to keep it that way.
- **[Jonas Dawn](../npcs/jonas-dawn.md)** — an old friend, greeted warmly by name. Icarus grabbed him by the shirt and threatened to kill him for information within minutes of sitting down.
- **The [Silverbrier head of security](../npcs/silverbrier-head-of-security.md)** — a running, mutual contempt. Icarus cannot negotiate with him, because every conversation ends with *"you were two weeks late."*
