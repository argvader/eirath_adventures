# Miriam Dawn

**Missing — [Church of the Dawn](../factions/church-of-the-dawn.md)**

## Overview

[Jonas](jonas-dawn.md)'s sister, and by every account a far better person than anyone else in [Timberfall](../locations/timberfall.md). When the notice went up about the twelve missing loggers, Miriam did not haggle and did not hesitate — she gathered three others and went into the [Graylight Forest](../locations/graylight-forest.md) to bring them home. That was about a week ago. Nobody has heard from her since.

The party has not met her. They have only met the hole she left.

## Personality

A do-gooder in the sincerest and most inconvenient sense. Her brother begged her to stay — there was more than enough work in town, he told her, patching up people who cut their own thumbs off — and she told him she had to go help those people, because she was better equipped to help than anyone else who might try. He could not argue her out of it.

She was not even going to keep the money. The whole fee was meant to fund the family's threadbare little temple.

## Her Company

She went out with three others, none of them named to the party so far:

- An **orc**, an upstart hedge knight.
- A **high elf** woman — not drow — who carried herself with the particular smugness elves are famous for. Said to come from one of Timberfall's founding families.
- A **gnome**, very flashy, very outspoken, with black or blue hair.

They negotiated **thirty-five gold per surviving logger** from the Silverbriers — considerably better than the twenty the party settled for. The [Silverbrier head of security](silverbrier-head-of-security.md) has made it clear that if this first party comes back with living loggers, they get paid and the party does not. He has also made it clear that the Silverbriers will pay nothing at all for rescuing *them*.

## The Trail

The party found her. Not her — her **tracks**, at [the logging camp](../locations/the-logging-camp.md), three sets of armored footprints laid down over the loggers' week-and-a-half-old boot marks and only a few days old themselves. Her group reached the cut site well ahead of the party.

They did not leave it together. The armored tracks come into the camp and then **split into two paths**, staggering off in different directions. One set is smaller than the other.

Wilonet chose to follow the smaller set on the assumption that it is Miriam — and on the reasoning that Miriam went in looking for the loggers, so finding her is the fastest way to find all of them. Icarus agreed, with a different reason: he knew [Jonas](jonas-dawn.md), and he trusts Jonas's sister further than he trusts *some random knight.*

## Status

**Missing, two weeks and change.** Alive or dead is unknown. Confirmed to have made it at least as far as the cut site.
