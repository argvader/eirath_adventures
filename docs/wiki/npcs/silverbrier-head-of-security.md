# The Silverbrier Head of Security

**Gate guard and contract-man — [Silverbrier Estate](../locations/silverbrier-estate.md)**

> His name has not yet come up. The party has never asked, and he has never offered.

## Overview

The human who stands at the gate of the [Silverbrier Estate](../locations/silverbrier-estate.md) and hands out the family's dirty work. He is the one who posted the notice about the twelve missing loggers, the one who set the price, and the one who will not be going into the [Graylight](../locations/graylight-forest.md) himself under any circumstances — a point he made repeatedly and with obvious satisfaction.

## Appearance

Middle-aged, salt-and-pepper hair, a goatee. Smug. He leans in when he talks, as though letting you in on something.

## Personality

Cheerfully contemptuous of just about everyone, and unbothered about hiding it. He calls the people he's hiring "brave people — idiots" in the same breath. He barely reads; the map he handed the party is, in his own words, drawn by a man who learned the letter X two weeks ago, and it looks like it. He told Icarus, without lowering his voice, that if the drow and the dwarf died out there the survivors could just keep their shares, and that he did not care in the slightest if they killed each other.

He also does not know what a lawsuit is. Possibly the lady of the house does.

## What He Wants

The loggers back at work — or failing that, back at all. Failing *that*, more loggers hired cheap out of **Deep Hollow**, where there are always more desperate people. He is not especially invested in which.

## Relationships

- **[Lady Silverbrier](../factions/silverbrier-family.md)** — his employer. He defers everything of consequence to her and clearly does not want to be the one making decisions.
- **Icarus** — has worked for the family before and was *supposed* to be the security escort for this very logging expedition. He turned up two weeks late. The head of security has not let this go for a single minute of their acquaintance, and used it to shut down Icarus's attempt to negotiate the rate up from twenty gold.

## Session History

**[Twelve Missing](../../sessions/2026-07-05.md)** — Hired the party at twenty gold per surviving logger, refused to budge, handed over a nearly useless map, and mentioned — almost in passing — that a *previous* rescue party of four had already gone out, and that if they came back with survivors first, the party would get nothing.
