# Cara

**Logger, missing — [the Cut Site](../locations/the-logging-camp.md), [Graylight Forest](../locations/graylight-forest.md)**

## Overview

An orcish woman hired off the Graylight's outer trade roads to swing an axe for the
[Silverbriers](../factions/silverbrier-family.md). [Halvik Dern](halvik-dern.md) recorded her
in his first journal entry the way a foreman records an asset: **strong arms, doesn't talk a
lot.** She is the first of the twelve he wrote down as a person and the first he wrote down as
a casualty, and the two entries are four days apart.

She is the only one of the missing loggers whose name anybody knows.

## What Happened to Her

Dern noticed her before he noticed anything else. As the crew's will to work drained away, he
wrote a single line about her that reads differently now than it must have then: **the orcish
woman's gone quiet.**

Then, mid-morning, mid-swing:

> *She pressed her forehead against the tree, did not speak, and then just began to bang her
> head against the tree. She started bleeding. We tried to check on her. She pushed us away —
> and she walked into the forest.*

**Two of the others followed her in.** Dern does not say whether they were trying to bring her
back.

## The Thing on Watch

Standing the second watch at the abandoned camp days later, [Icarus](../pcs/icarus.md) saw an
orc woman walk out of the trees. He was reaching over to wake the others when he registered
that she was **translucent** — and that whatever she was shrugging off her shoulders was not
there either. She went to a tree and began driving her head into it, exactly as the journal
describes.

The tree splintered. Chunks came off it under blows that had no body behind them. In the
morning the damage was still there in the bark, and no one could tell how old it was.

Nobody in the party has said out loud whether that means she is dead.

## Status

**Missing.** Walked into the [Graylight](../locations/graylight-forest.md) under her own power,
bleeding, sometime in the first week of the cut.
