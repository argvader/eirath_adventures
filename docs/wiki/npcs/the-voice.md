# The Voice

**[Icarus](../pcs/icarus.md)'s patron — unnamed, unseen, resident**

## Overview

Something is in Icarus's head. It speaks to him in the first person plural, it addresses him
by name, it wants to be fed, and it has never once been described out loud to another member
of the party.

It has no name yet. Icarus calls it nothing. The one time anyone at the table named it, they
called it **the demon in your head**, and Icarus did not argue.

## What It Has Said

Every line of it, in order of when Icarus heard it:

> *Good. We don't want the light.* — unbidden, in answer to a
> [Church of the Dawn](../factions/church-of-the-dawn.md) blessing Icarus had already
> refused out loud.

> *I need sustenance. You will give me what I need, Icarus. We are bound together.* — on the
> road to [Timberfall](../locations/timberfall.md), for days.

> *Wake up, Icarus. Wake up. You need to see something. Go downstairs.*

> *You must stop him. He cannot do this anymore. Be brave, Icarus. I will guide you.*

> *Kill him. Make sure he stops, Icarus. Slice his throat and sustain me.*

> *We can be strong, Icarus. Slay this man, and I will grant you more power.*

> *We will keep doing this, Icarus, but you will keep getting stronger. And if you don't,
> **your mother's life is forfeit.***

## What It Does

Three things, consistently:

- **It feeds.** It asked for sustenance for days before it got any, and what it wanted was a
  death — a throat cut, in its words, to *sustain* it. When Icarus killed
  [the old farmer](the-old-farmer.md), the blood ran up the sword and **into** the blade.
- **It pays.** As Icarus came down those stairs he felt his arms thicken and his footing go
  light, and the magic he had never managed to cast before came out of his hand
  unprompted and took the farmer's face off. The power is real, it arrives at the moment of
  the kill, and it is explicitly offered as a wage.
- **It lies, or lets him lie to himself.** Everything the voice sent him downstairs to see was
  wrong. The farmer's monstrous arms went back to being an old man's. The human corpse on the
  block was a deer. The voice showed Icarus a monster and collected a life the instant he
  swung at it.

## The Leverage

The threat against **Icarus's mother** is the first time it has asked for something rather
than offered something, and the first evidence that it knows anything about his life outside
his own skull. Whoever she is and wherever she is, she is now the price of him refusing.

## What Nobody Knows

Icarus has told **no one**. Coming out of the flashback shaking in front of the other two, he
said he was fine and that he was *just thinking of the past.* Later, when the party finally
compared their dreams, this is as far as he went:

> *I saw my past. The moment I don't speak of. Never.*

[Wilonet](../pcs/wilonet.md) — who has had no visions at all — warned him that the forest has
a magic in it and told him not to let it affect him. She had no way of knowing that the thing
talking to him was not the forest.
