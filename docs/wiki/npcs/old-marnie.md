# Old Marnie

**Alchemist — [Ol' Marnie's](../locations/ol-marnies.md), [Timberfall](../locations/timberfall.md)**

## Overview

An elf, and old even by the standards of elves — which in a town where a three-hundred-year-old drow counts as the ancient one is saying something. He keeps a cramped alchemical hut at the edge of [Timberfall](../locations/timberfall.md), brews the town's potions, and forages out in the fringes of the [Graylight Forest](../locations/graylight-forest.md) for the things he needs. He has been here a very long time and he knows more about those woods than anyone else still breathing in this town.

## Personality

Dry, unhurried, faintly amused by everyone. He greeted Wilonet as an old friend and Grano politely, and dismissed Icarus with a shrug and the observation that *"he's young — most humans are."* He is not frightened of the Graylight the way Jonas is. He simply respects it, which is worse.

## What He Knows

Old Marnie is the party's best source on the forest, and what he gave them was this:

- The Graylight **fucks with your head**. It makes you see things that were never there.
- The oldest rumor, and the one he clearly credits: **the forest does not want to be cut down.** The spirits in it have been dormant a long time. Sometimes it bites back.
- **The deeper you go, the fewer people have been there — and the angrier the spirits get.**
- His parting advice, and the only thing he'd call hard: **don't trust everything you see out there.**

## Nightroot

He asked the party to bring back any **nightroot** they come across — a dark blue, vine-like plant that grows out in the Graylight. He can "turn it into something." He drew them a sketch of it before they left. He did not say what it becomes, and nobody thought to ask.

**They have it.** A good bundle, harvested deep in the forest on a 24. It cost more than he could have known: the first nightroot they found was a **Nightroot Asp** wearing the plant's shape, and it opened Grano's throat and put him on the ground before Icarus killed it. The real root was lying a few feet from the snake that had been imitating it, and Wilonet flatly refused to be the one to cut it the second time.

Marnie does not yet know any of this. He also does not know they are bringing him four vials of the asp's venom.

## Relationships

- **Wilonet** — a genuine old friend. She has known him for years and vouched for him to the others; she asked to do the talking, and asked the others, pointedly, not to rough him up.
- **[Jonas Dawn](jonas-dawn.md)** — he supplies Jonas's potions and helps him brew them. He gave Jonas most of his remaining stock days before the party arrived, which is why he had none to spare when they asked.
- **[Miriam Dawn](miriam-dawn.md)** — she came to him asking about the forest the day before she left. He knows exactly how that story is likely to end.
- **Icarus** — spent the conversation eyeing the shelves and sizing up what he could steal, and has stated aloud that he intends to try. Marnie has not noticed. Yet.
