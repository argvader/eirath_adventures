# The Church of the Dawn

**A faith of redemption, rebirth, and beginning again**

## Overview

A church whose whole theology is the sunrise: **redemption, rebirth, starting anew.** Whatever you were before does not have to be what you are now. In a world built out of the corpses of a war between demons and celestials — where every race alive carries some measure of that old blood in it — a faith about starting over is not a small idea.

Grano, examining the potions they were handed, recognized it immediately and knew it well.

## Sigil

A **half-risen sun** on a white field, small rays coming off it. [Jonas Dawn](../npcs/jonas-dawn.md) wears it as a pendant over his robes.

## Presence in Timberfall

Small and poor. The [Dawn family](../npcs/jonas-dawn.md) has been in [Timberfall](../locations/timberfall.md) a long time and runs a meager little temple there. It is not a wealthy institution — [Miriam Dawn](../npcs/miriam-dawn.md) took the logger-rescue contract intending to hand the *entire* fee over to fund it, and Jonas gives away the healing potions he brews with [Old Marnie](../npcs/old-marnie.md) rather than sell them.

Their blessing, offered in parting: **"May the light find you."**

## Known Members

- **[Jonas Dawn](../npcs/jonas-dawn.md)** — healer, brewer of potions, and the one who says the dawn gives him power. It may not be a figure of speech.
- **[Miriam Dawn](../npcs/miriam-dawn.md)** — his sister. Missing in the [Graylight Forest](../locations/graylight-forest.md).

## Relationship with the Party

The church has given the party healing potions and asked nothing for them. It is, so far, the only institution in Timberfall that has treated them decently — which did not stop Icarus from grabbing its local priest by the shirt and threatening to kill him.

**And there is something else.** When Jonas offered Icarus the church's blessing — *"may the light find you"* — Icarus said he didn't care about the light. And then, unbidden, in his own skull, he heard a thought that was not his:

> *Good. We don't want the light.*

Whatever Icarus's patron is, it has opinions about this church.
