# The Silverbrier Estate

A gated manor house that is, by a comfortable margin, the finest building in [Timberfall](timberfall.md) — which in Timberfall is not saying a great deal, but the gate is real and the guard standing at it is real. Everyone in town knows where it is. The [Silverbrier family](../factions/silverbrier-family.md) runs its logging concern out of it, and the job board where the party first laid eyes on one another stands within sight of the gate.

## The Posting

> **MISSING — 12 LOGGERS**
> from the Silverbrier family. **Pays well.**
> Interested persons should inquire.

It does not pay well. The [head of security](../npcs/silverbrier-head-of-security.md) who stands at the gate settled the party at twenty gold a head for every logger brought back alive, and would not be moved off it — pointedly noting that Icarus, who was supposed to have been escorting those loggers in the first place and turned up two weeks late, had no leverage whatsoever.

He was also perfectly happy to observe that if the party's members killed each other out there, the survivors could simply keep the dead one's cut.

## Who Holds It

**Lady Silverbrier** — a second daughter, and therefore a woman with no inheritance coming to her. She was sent out to Timberfall to run the family's logging interest, largely against her will; she would much rather be in **Deep Hollow**. She is described, by people who have to live near her, as greedy and entirely indifferent to the common folk. It was her call to send twelve loggers into the [Graylight](graylight-forest.md) with no escort rather than wait two more days and pay them for the delay.

The party has not yet met her. She has not come out to the gate.
