# Ol' Marnie's

A cramped alchemical hut on the edge of [Timberfall](timberfall.md), barely more than a shack, with a hand-lettered sign reading **OL' MARNIE'S** and shelves of glass crowding every wall. It is the only place in town to buy a potion, and lately there is very little on the shelves worth buying — [Old Marnie](../npcs/old-marnie.md) gave most of his remaining stock to [Jonas Dawn](../npcs/jonas-dawn.md) days ago, and says he has nothing left to spare.

## What's On the Shelves

Mostly empty space and dust. But two bottles caught Icarus's eye while he loitered by the shelves, pretending not to case the room:

- Potions matching the ones Jonas had been handing out — ordinary healing.
- **An odd, near-empty bottle.** It looks like it holds nothing at all, until you squint: there is a little clear liquid in the bottom, and it *moves* when the light from outside catches it. Icarus studied it and came away with no idea what it was. It has not been identified, and it has not been stolen.

Both Wilonet and the shopkeeper made the consequences of theft in a small town extremely clear. Icarus's position, stated aloud, is that he is probably going to try anyway.

## What Happened Here

The party came to Old Marnie for information about the [Graylight Forest](graylight-forest.md), on Jonas's recommendation. They got the local wisdom — the fog lies to you, the forest doesn't want to be cut, the spirits get angrier the deeper you go — and a standing request: bring back any **nightroot** he can turn into something useful. He drew them a picture of it before they left.
