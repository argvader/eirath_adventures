# The Fat Goat

[Timberfall](timberfall.md)'s one and only tavern — a ramshackle, leaning thing hung with a painted sign of an enormous white goat split cleanly in half. It smells overwhelmingly of mutton, which is also, unsurprisingly, what they sell. Gnomes help each other up onto barstools that were not built for them. A half-elf works behind the bar, endlessly polishing the same glass and staring at nothing in particular.

## Who Drinks Here

Everyone, because there is nowhere else. On the night the party came through, the notable patron was [Jonas Dawn](../npcs/jonas-dawn.md) — the town healer, sitting alone at a table with an ale at his elbow, one leg jittering under him hard enough that a sharp ear could hear it across the room. He was not drinking because he wanted to.

## What Happened Here

The party met for the first time at the job board and came here to ask questions before heading into the [Graylight](graylight-forest.md). They got their first real thread: Jonas's sister **Miriam** had gone into the forest with three others roughly a week ago, and had not come back.

They also got their first taste of each other. Icarus grabbed the town healer by the shirt and threatened to kill him. Wilonet — who had heard every word of Jonas's muttering from clear across the room — announced to the whole tavern that the price for rescuing his sister had just gone up to forty gold. It went about as well as that sounds, and Jonas still handed over healing potions on his way out, with instructions about who not to waste one on.
