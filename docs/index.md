# Eirath Adventures

Chronicles of blood and iron from the world of Eirath.

This is the session archive and campaign wiki for **Eirath Adventures** — a
record of who the party is, what they've done, and the world that keeps testing
them. It's a fresh campaign: the pages below fill in as sessions are played and
summarized.

## Getting Started

1. Fill in `world/world.md` with your setting — nations, regions, factions, and
   pantheon.
2. Record and transcribe your first session (see `README.md`), then drop the
   materials into `sessions-raw/<DATE>/`.
3. Run the **summarize latest session** skill to generate the session page and
   wiki entries, and **build world data** to build this home page.
4. **publish site** to push it live.
